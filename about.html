Our vision is to make good teachers accessible to everyone

Throughout high school and college we struggled with finding teachers that we wanted to take classes with. And even in university we couldn't find ratings for professors. Most people found out about them through friends and family, and what might be the best fit for one person might not work for another.

We want to make sure that every student finds the teacher they want, according to their learning styles and specific needs. And from that, Sirkoi was born.
